To create this beloved sauce, we partnered with a supplier located in Puglia (the heel of the “boot”), where the hot, dry summers and mild, wet winters of this Mediterranean region are ideal for growing tomatoes...

Authentic Italian Olive Oil

Cold-pressed from hand-picked olives, this extra virgin olive oil delivers a rich, fruity taste. Perfect for drizzling over salads, dipping bread, or cooking classic Italian dishes.

Freshly Made Pasta

Crafted with durum wheat semolina and fresh spring water, our pasta is slow-dried for a firm texture and authentic taste — just like traditional Italian kitchens.

Classic Italian Balsamic Vinegar

Aged in wooden barrels in Modena, this balsamic vinegar offers a perfect balance of sweetness and tang, ideal for marinades, dressings, and gourmet finishing.

Rustic Italian Bread

Baked with a slow fermentation process, this rustic loaf has a crispy crust and soft, airy interior — perfect for enjoying with olive oil and sauces.
